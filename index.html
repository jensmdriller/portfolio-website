Data Warehouse - [Project Details](#data-warehouse)

By consolidating relational and NoSQL databases into one location, the scientists could then analyze results and preform analytics on the data. Before long, this data warehouse made it's way into every major lab process leading to greater discoveries and as a result, high valuation for the company as a whole.

Cloud Migration

You don't know how painfully bad AWS is until you have a project deployed on Google's Cloud Platform (GCP). At least, that's how I felt after my first time developing on GCP. Before long, the developers at the company realized this too and made the executive decision to migrate all our services over. I was asked to lead this effort and in only 3 weeks, all essential services were opeational on Google Cloud. Other than some cold storage left on S3, we were fully operational on GCP and couldn't be happier.

Nest Camera - API Alerts - [git repo](#git-rename)

My family wanted a tool to recieve alerts if there was a lack of motion detected by a specific Nest Camera. I know right, 99% of people want to know when there **is** motion, not when there is **not**. The reason though was because my Grandma lives alone and the family was worried about the possiblity of her falling down. Thus, if too much time went by without motion, the script triggers an email alert to a group of family members.

Also, Nest, if y'all are reading this, you need to release an API SDK.
